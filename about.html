What is Studio Raindrops?

"Studio Raindrops" is a multi-media entertainment site. We create illustrations, video games, animations, music, and any other projects we feel like creating. In addition, this site will be the central hub for all the artist contributing to this site. Currently, the website is still in development and will probably always be in development. Therefore, please understand that some features may not work as intended and the entire site is subject to change without warning.

Thank you for your visit and we hope you enjoy your stay!

-Studio Raindrops
