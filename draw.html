Draw!

Info

Draw! is a reaction game that rewards patience.
Wait for the opportune time to shoot your opponent.
Created by Summer Rain for CISGAME2 using the Construct 3 engine.


Rules & Controls
Use your finger or mouse to navigate through the game.
Whenever the game tells you to tap, you can tap a touch interface or left click a mouse.
Wait for the perfect time to shoot our opponent.
